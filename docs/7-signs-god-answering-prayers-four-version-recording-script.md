## VERSION 1

# 7 Signs God Is Answering Your Prayers

## INTRO

Thank you for clicking on this video.

And honestly...

if you are here right now...

there is a good chance you are in a season where you have been praying for something.

Maybe for a long time.

Maybe every single night.

Maybe quietly.

Maybe desperately.

You have been asking God for direction.

For help.

For healing.

For peace.

For clarity.

Maybe it is a relationship.

Maybe it is your health.

Maybe it is financial stress.

Maybe you are trying to figure out what to do with your life.

Maybe you are praying for your family.

Maybe you are just tired.

And if we are being real...

sometimes one of the hardest parts about praying...

is not knowing if God is even listening.

Because sometimes you pray...

and nothing changes immediately.

The situation is still there.

The pain is still there.

The confusion is still there.

And you start wondering:

"God... are You even hearing me?"

But here is something important to understand before this video even begins:

Just because God is silent...

does not mean God is absent.

And just because the answer has not come the way you expected...

does not mean He is not moving.

Because sometimes...

God answers prayers in ways we do not recognize at first.

Sometimes the answer comes through:

closed doors

delays

conviction

discomfort

peace

redirection

growth

And if you do not know what to look for...

you might completely miss the fact that God is already responding.

And honestly, one reason many people struggle to recognize when God is moving...

is because they spend very little time in God's Word.

Because the more time spent in Scripture...

the easier it becomes to recognize God's character, His direction, and His voice.

And if you want help studying the Bible consistently and understanding God's Word more deeply, you can sign up free at mybiblebuddy.net.

So over the next few minutes...

let's walk through 7 signs God may already be answering your prayers...

even if it does not look the way you expected.

And by the end of this video...

the hope is that you stop feeling forgotten...

and start realizing God may be working behind the scenes more than you think.

## SIGN 1

## Doors Start Closing

Revelation 3:7 says:

"What He opens no one can shut, and what He shuts no one can open."

This was written by John while he was receiving visions from Jesus about the churches.

And in this passage...

Jesus is reminding people that He is sovereign.

Meaning:

He sees what we cannot see.

And He has authority over doors we do not understand.

Now bring that into real life.

One of the hardest things to accept...

is that sometimes God answers prayers by closing doors.

You prayed for the opportunity.

But it fell apart.

You prayed for the relationship.

But it ended.

You prayed for the job.

But they rejected you.

And at first...

it feels like disappointment.

Failure.

Rejection.

But sometimes what feels like rejection...

is actually protection.

Because people often thank God for the things He gave them...

but rarely thank Him for the things He protected them from.

And the truth is...

there are some doors you wanted to walk through...

that would have destroyed your peace.

Destroyed your focus.

Destroyed your future.

But you could not see it at the time.

So God shut the door.

And that is hard.

Especially when you really wanted it.

But one thing that is true is this:

God sees the full picture.

We only see the moment.

Sometimes people cry over doors God closed...

while God is protecting them from things they would have cried even harder about later.

And maybe right now...

you are frustrated because something did not work out.

But maybe...

just maybe...

God is answering your prayer by redirecting your path.

## SIGN 2

## Your Desires Start Changing

Psalm 37:4 says:

"Delight yourself in the Lord, and He will give you the desires of your heart."

A lot of people misunderstand this verse.

They think it means:

"If I follow God, He gives me everything I want."

But sometimes...

the first thing God changes...

is what you want.

Because before God changes your situation...

He often changes your heart.

You start caring about different things.

Things that used to satisfy you...

do not hit the same anymore.

You start wanting peace more than attention.

Purpose more than popularity.

Character more than comfort.

And honestly...

sometimes that process feels uncomfortable.

Because God starts pulling you away from things:

habits

relationships

environments

lifestyles

that no longer align with where He is taking you.

And you may not even fully understand why.

You just know:

"I do not feel the same anymore."

That is not random.

That can be God reshaping your heart.

Because answered prayer is not always:

"God, give me what I want."

Sometimes it is:

"God, transform me into who I need to become."

And that is a deeper answer than most people realize.

## SIGN 3

## You Feel Convicted, Not Comfortable

Hebrews 12:6 says:

"The Lord disciplines the one He loves."

That verse can sound harsh at first.

But discipline in the Bible is not punishment.

It is correction.

Guidance.

Growth.

If God truly did not care about you...

He would let you destroy yourself without conviction.

But when you start feeling convicted...

when certain things stop sitting right in your spirit...

that can actually be a sign God is working on you.

You start realizing:

"I should not be doing this."

"I need to change."

"This lifestyle is not healthy."

"I need to get closer to God."

And conviction feels different than shame.

Shame says:

"You are worthless."

Conviction says:

"You were made for more than this."

That is a huge difference.

And a lot of people pray for God to change their lives...

but get frustrated when God starts changing them first.

But transformation starts internally.

Before God changes your environment...

He often changes your heart.

## SIGN 4

## You Are Forced To Slow Down

Isaiah 40:31 says:

"Those who wait on the Lord shall renew their strength."

Waiting is one of the hardest spiritual lessons.

Because nobody likes waiting.

People want answers now.

Movement now.

Breakthrough now.

But sometimes...

God slows things down on purpose.

Not because He forgot you.

But because He is preparing you.

And honestly...

some of the biggest mistakes people make happen because they moved too fast.

Too fast emotionally.

Too fast financially.

Too fast spiritually.

Too fast relationally.

Sometimes slowing down is mercy.

And maybe right now...

you feel stuck.

But maybe God is using this season to:

mature you

strengthen you

heal you

prepare you

teach you dependence

Because some things only grow in waiting seasons.

Patience.

Faith.

Trust.

Character.

And while waiting feels painful...

it often produces deeper roots.

## SIGN 5

## You Start Letting Go Of Certain Things

Ecclesiastes 3:1 says:

"There is a time for everything."

One sign God is moving in your life...

is you start releasing things you once clung to.

Sometimes it is people.

Sometimes it is habits.

Sometimes it is old versions of yourself.

And honestly...

letting go is painful.

Because even unhealthy things can become familiar.

But growth often requires release.

And sometimes God removes things...

not to hurt you...

but because they cannot go where He is taking you.

You start realizing:

certain conversations drain you

certain habits hold you back

certain environments damage your peace

And slowly...

your priorities start shifting.

That is not random.

That is growth.

## SIGN 6

## You Have Peace That Does Not Make Sense

Philippians 4:7 says:

"The peace of God, which surpasses all understanding..."

This kind of peace is different.

Because it does not always match the situation.

The bills may still exist.

The uncertainty may still exist.

The answer may still not be visible.

But somehow...

you still feel calm.

That is one of the clearest signs God is near.

Because real peace is not the absence of problems.

It is the presence of God in the middle of them.

And there are moments where nothing externally changed...

but internally...

you know:

"God is with me."

That peace matters.

Do not ignore it.

## SIGN 7

## You Keep Getting Pulled Back To God

James 4:8 says:

"Draw near to God and He will draw near to you."

One of the biggest signs God is answering you...

is you keep feeling pulled back toward Him.

Even after mistakes.

Even after drifting.

Even after doubt.

You keep hearing:

sermons

verses

conversations

reminders

that point you back to Him.

That is not coincidence.

Sometimes God answers prayer by drawing you closer first.

Because the greatest answer is not always:

money

relationships

opportunities

Sometimes the greatest answer...

is intimacy with Him.

Because when you truly know God...

everything else starts changing too.

## OUTRO

So if you have been praying...

and wondering if God hears you...

let this be a reminder of something important:

God may already be answering you...

just not in the way you expected.

Sometimes He answers through:

closed doors

conviction

waiting

peace

redirection

growth

drawing you closer

And maybe right now...

instead of asking:

"God, where are You?"

Maybe the better question is:

"God... what are You trying to show me in this season?"

Because He has not forgotten you.

And if you are still breathing...

God is still working.

Even in silence.

Even in uncertainty.
